nl.han.ica.app

Assess Service

The Assess Service is responsible for (automatically) assessing exams.
Requests are made through a REST endpoint. The Assess Service will get the exams from a database
together with the answer models. From there the exams will be assessed and stored temporarily in a Redis database,
waiting for a teacher to review the (automatic) assessment. After the teacher has reviewed the assessment the exam
will be archived in a MongoDB database.
